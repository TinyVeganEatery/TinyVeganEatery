Ingredients for the Best Sourdough Truffle Toast:

  • 1 slice of sourdough bread (toasted to perfection)
  • 2 tablespoons of cashew cheese
  • 1 teaspoon nutritional yeast
  • Salt (I used this smoked salt)
  • Pepper to taste
  • ½ teaspoon truffle oil (a little goes a long way)

How to Make Sourdough Truffle Toast:

  1. Toast your sourdough bread: Start by toasting your slice of sourdough bread until it's golden brown and crisp. A good quality sourdough will give your toast a slightly tangy flavor and chewy texture.
  2. Spread the cashew cheese: Generously spread 2 tablespoons of creamy cashew cheese on top of the toasted bread. The cashew cheese provides a delicious, dairy-free base for your toast.
  3. Add nutritional yeast: Sprinkle 1 teaspoon of nutritional yeast evenly over the cashew cheese. Nutritional yeast adds a cheesy, nutty flavor that perfectly complements the richness of the truffle oil.
  4. Season with salt and pepper: Lightly season the toast with a pinch of salt and freshly ground black pepper for added flavor.
  5. Drizzle with truffle oil: Finish by drizzling a small amount of truffle oil over the top. The truffle oil adds a luxurious, earthy aroma and elevates the flavors to the next level.
  6. Serve immediately: Enjoy your sourdough truffle toast fresh and warm for the best flavor and texture!
